Features

Everything between “capture” and “done”

One extension handles the screenshot, the cleanup and the export — no extra apps, no image editors.

Full-page capture

Auto-scrolls the page and stitches every frame into one seamless image — sticky headers deduplicated, lazy-loaded content waited for, Retina displays handled.

Visible-area capture

Just need what's on screen? Grab the current viewport with one click and jump straight into the editor.

Crop & edit

Trim with freeform or fixed ratios (1:1, 4:3, 16:9), zoom and pan giant captures smoothly, and undo/redo every step.

Export your way

Save as PNG, JPEG, WebP or a paginated PDF. Pick the resolution, see a live size estimate, and get smart date-stamped file names.

Copy to clipboard

Send the finished screenshot straight to your clipboard for chat, docs or email — or let it auto-save to your Downloads folder.

Private by default

No servers, no analytics, no remote code. Built on Manifest V3 — everything runs in your browser and nothing ever leaves it.
